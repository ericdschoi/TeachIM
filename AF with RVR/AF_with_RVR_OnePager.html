ATRIAL FIBRILLATION WITH RVR — Clinical One-Pager

Irregularly irregular, narrow QRS, no P waves, rate >100 · 2023 ACC/AHA/ACCP/HRS · IM resident reference

1 · RECOGNIZE

AF w/ RVR = irregularly irregular rhythm · narrow QRS (<120 ms) · no discernible P waves · ventricular rate >100. Atria fire ~400 bpm; AV-node refractory period gates the rate (→ AV-nodal blockers work).
Wide-complex AF only with underlying BBB or rate-related aberrancy — irregular irregularity still points to AF.

2 · WORK-UP (new AF)

Universal: TTE, BMP + Mg, TSH (± reflex T4), CBC, ECG ± troponin. Order the rest in context.
CardiacNon-cardiac
Valvular / structural HD; decompensated HF (±BNP); ischemia (trop)Pulmonary/PE (±CXR); OSA screen; drugs/EtOH (±tox); electrolytes; hyperthyroid

3 · STABILITY FIRST

UNSTABLE?Action
YESSBP <90, or SBP↓ >30 + [CP / AMS / SOB] from rhythm → synchronized DCCV 120-200 J (sedate if able, don't delay) + anticoag
NORate control to HR <110. Acute HR >130 → IV meds; HR 110-130 → PO meds
Lenient HR <110 acceptable if asymptomatic + preserved EF (RACE II).

4 · RATE-CONTROL AGENTS

AgentLine / dose / caution
Metoprolol (BB)1st; IV 5 mg q5min ×3, PO 12.5-37.5 q6h; ↓↓BP, caution decomp HFrEF/sepsis
Diltiazem (CCB)1st; IV 10-20 mg bolus+drip, PO 30-90 q6h; ↓↓BP, AVOID HFrEF
Digoxin2nd; IV 0.25-0.5 mg load → 0.25 q6h; BP-neutral + inotropy; caution elderly/renal
Amiodarone2nd; IV 150 mg bolus + drip; favored in shock; caution chronic AF (may cardiovert); lung/liver/thyroid tox
Fail one agent → add a second class (e.g., CCB on top of BB).

SCENARIO PICKS

  • HFrEF: avoid CCB; use BB once compensated; digoxin/amiodarone OK
  • Septic shock / hypotension: amiodarone (most BP-neutral); digoxin alt
  • BP-limited after BB: digoxin (BP-neutral + inotropy)
  • Unstable / decompensating: synchronized DCCV

5 · ANTICOAGULATION

Cardioversion + AF ≥48 h or unknown duration: ≥3 wk therapeutic anticoag before OR TEE to exclude thrombus → then ≥4 wk after, regardless of score (atrial stunning).
Long-term stroke prophylaxis by CHA2DS2-VASc (OAC if ≥2 M / ≥3 F). 2024 ESC: CHA2DS2-VA (drops sex). Anticoagulate everyone cardioverted.
