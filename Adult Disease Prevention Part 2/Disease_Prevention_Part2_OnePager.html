Activity: ≥150–300 min/wk moderate (or 75–150 vigorous) + strengthening ×2/wk.

2026 ACC/AHA dyslipidemia

Use PREVENT (30–79), not Pooled Cohort (overest. ~40–50%). Tiers: low <3% · border 3–<5% · interm 5–<10% · high ≥10%. "Earlier & lower, for longer."

LDL targets: very-high <55, high <70, interm-on-statin <100. Earlier statin if FH, ≤30 w/ LDL ≥160, strong family ASCVD, or high 30-yr risk.

Vaccine note (2024)

PCV21 now an option for adults ≥50 (cases say PCV20 — still valid). RSV for ≥75 (and 60–74 high-risk) by shared decision.

Framework
