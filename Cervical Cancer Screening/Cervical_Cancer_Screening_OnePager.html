Cervical Cancer Screening — Clinical Reference

Persistent hrHPV (16/18) → >90% at transformation zone · TeachIM.org (Dec 2022, optimized Jun 2026) · CC BY-NC 4.0 · educational use only — clinician-review before teaching

1 · Natural history

  • Persistent high-risk HPV (chiefly 16, 18) causes nearly all cervical cancer; ~90% of infections clear within 2 y, persistent minority → precancer over years.
  • >90% arise at the transformation zone (squamocolumnar junction) — sample it. Ectocervix (squamous) → SCC ~75%; endocervix (columnar) → adenocarcinoma ~25%.
  • Persistence risk ↑: older age, oncogenic subtype, immunosuppression, smoking, other STIs, ?cOCP.

2 · Average-risk screening

AgeOptions
21–29Cytology q3y (no HPV testing <30)
30–65Primary hrHPV q5y (USPSTF 2025 / ACS preferred) — or co-test q5y, or cytology q3y
Stop>65 if no CIN2+ x25y + adequate prior screen (3 neg cytol/10y or 2 neg HPV/10y, most recent <3/5y)
Post-hystStop after total hysterectomy (cervix out) for benign cause + no CIN2+ x25y
  • HPV vaccination does NOT change the schedule. Positive primary HPV → reflex cytology, same specimen.

3 · High-risk (immunosuppressed)

  • HIV, transplant, SLE, IBD/RA on immunosuppression, chronic steroids.
  • HIV: <30 cytology only (baseline → annual ×3 → q3y); ≥30 cytology or co-test q3y; continue for life (no stop at 65). Primary HPV-with-reflex NOT recommended.
  • Non-HIV immunosuppressed → follow CDC HIV protocol. IBD/RA off immunosuppression or T1DM → general-population guideline.
  • Lower threshold for colposcopy: ASC-US/HPV+, or LSIL/ASC-H/AGC/HSIL regardless of HPV.

4 · Manage abnormal results (ASCCP app)

Immediate CIN3+ riskAction
<4%Surveillance by 5-y risk: >0.55%→1y; 0.15–0.55%→3y; <0.15%→5y
4–24%Colposcopy
25–59%Expedited tx or colposcopy (per future-pregnancy desire)
60–100%Expedited treatment (excision > ablation)
  • Cytology (↑ severity): NILM → ASC-US → LSIL → ASC-H → AGC → HSIL. Histology: No CIN → CIN1 → CIN2-3 → AIS.
  • Excision (LEEP/cone) preferred over ablation for histologic HSIL & AIS (yields tissue).
  • Hx CIN2+: HPV-based at 6 mo → 3 annual → q3y for ≥25 y (even >65).

5 · 2025 update (framework unchanged)

  • USPSTF 2025 (Grade A): primary hrHPV q5y preferred for 30–65; cytology q3y still an option.
  • First-time endorsement of FDA-approved self-collected hrHPV (CIN2+ Sn ~0.86) — access expansion.
  • ACS 2025: start at 25 with primary hrHPV; self-collection endorsed.
