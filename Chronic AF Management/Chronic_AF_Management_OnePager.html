Chronic Atrial Fibrillation Management — Clinical One-Pager

Stroke prevention · rate control · rhythm control. Framing per 2023 ACC/AHA/ACCP/HRS AF guideline. Adapted from TeachIM.org (CC BY-NC 4.0). Educational use only.

1 · Stroke risk → anticoagulate

CHA₂DS₂-VASc: CHF 1 · HTN 1 · Age 65-74 =1 / ≥75 =2 · DM 1 · Stroke/TIA/embolism 2 · Vascular dz 1 · Sex (female) 1.
Anticoagulate if annual stroke risk ≥2%/yr (Class 1) ≈ ≥2 men / ≥3 women. Score 1(m)/2(f) = reasonable (2a). Use score to estimate risk, not as rigid cutoff. ASA not for stroke prevention. Any generalist can start it.

2 · Pick the anticoagulant

SituationChoice

Can't take OAC long-termLAAO/WATCHMAN (2a)
AS, MR, bioprosthetic = non-valvular → DOAC OK. Apixaban reduce to 2.5 mg BID if ≥2 of: age ≥80, wt ≤60 kg, Cr ≥1.5.

Don't withhold OAC for…

High HAS-BLED or frequent falls (alone). High bleeding-risk pts often gain more absolute benefit; ~295 falls/yr needed to outweigh. HAS-BLED: HTN(uncontrolled) · Abn renal/liver · Stroke · Bleeding hx · Labile INR · Elderly>65 · Drugs/EtOH.

Cardioversion rule

AF >48 h / unknown → 3 wk OAC or TEE before, then 4 wk OAC after regardless of CHA₂DS₂-VASc (atrial stunning).

3 · Rate control

Goal HR <110 bpm most (RACE II); lower if symptomatic / declining LV / tachy-cardiomyopathy.
  • Beta-blocker (metoprolol succinate, carvedilol, bisoprolol in HFrEF)
  • Diltiazem (non-DHP CCB) — contraindicated in HFrEF
  • Digoxin adjunct (HFrEF; renally cleared, watch tox)
  • Refractory → AV-node ablation + pacemaker

4 · Rhythm control

Favored by: symptoms attributable to AF (strongest), younger age, few comorbidities, recent onset, can't rate control, tachy-cardiomyopathy, LV dysfunction.
2023: catheter ablation Class 1 first-line in selected paroxysmal AF & in AF+HFrEF.
AAD tox: amiodarone → lung/liver/thyroid (PFT/LFT/TFT); sotalol & dofetilide → QT/Torsades (inpatient start, renal dosing); flecainide → avoid in structural/CAD.

2023 staging & risk-factor pillar

Stage 1 at-risk → 2 pre-AF → 3 AF (3A parox / 3B persistent / 3C long-standing / 3D post-ablation) → 4 permanent. Lifestyle: wt loss BMI <27, ↓/stop alcohol, BP & lipids, OSA screen, tobacco cessation.
