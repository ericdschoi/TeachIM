GENDER AFFIRMING CARE — Clinical One-Pager

Adult outpatient GAHT · WPATH SOC8 (2022) & Endocrine Society (2017) · IM resident reference

1 · TERMINOLOGY & AFFIRMING INTAKE

Four independent axes: gender identity (internal) · gender expression (outward) · sex assigned at birth (AMAB/AFAB; DSD formerly "intersex") · sexual orientation (attraction).
Ask aboutPatient-centered phrasing
Name"What name do you go by?" — avoid deadnaming
Pronouns"What pronouns do you use?" (he / she / they / ze)
Gender ID"How would you describe your current gender identity?"
Sex @ birth"What sex was assigned to you at birth?"
Organ inv."What organs do you have / have had removed?" — drives screening
If you misgender: Recognize → Apologize → Commit to change → Move on (briefly).

2 · EVALUATION — INFORMED CONSENT

Dx gender dysphoria (DSM-5) can be made by a generalist. Gender incongruence is not a mental illness. No mandatory MH gatekeeper to start hormones; SOC8 = one letter for surgery.
  • Capacity — understands risks/benefits/reversibility
  • Contraindications — e.g. estrogen-sensitive cancer, high VTE risk (estrogen)
  • Expectations — changes, timeline, what's irreversible
  • Fertility counseling — offer banking BEFORE starting
Options span: GAHT, surgery, voice therapy, mental health — guided by patient goals in an interdisciplinary medical home.

3 · FEMINIZING THERAPY

Two jobs: add estrogen + lower/block testosterone. Goal estradiol 100–200 pg/mL; total T <50 ng/dL.
DrugNotes / side effects
17β-estradiolPO / SL / patch / IM. VTE (patch = lowest risk); estrogen-dependent cancer = contra
Spironolactone#1 anti-androgen. ↑K⁺, orthostasis, ↑urination, ↓libido
5α-reductase inhAnti-androgen; usually with spiro
GnRH agonistPotent suppression; cost; bone health
Timeline: ↓erections/libido + fat redistrib ~3 mo · breast growth 2–5 y (irreversible) · ↓body hair 6–12 mo (often incomplete).

4 · MASCULINIZING THERAPY

Testosterone (single agent): IM / SC / topical. Goal trough total T 400–700 ng/dL. Key risk: erythrocytosis (check Hct → r/o OSA; ↓dose / ↑freq / →topical).
Timeline: ↑acne/oil + ↑libido early · voice deepening (irreversible) · menses cessation ~70% (~30% persist) · ↑muscle + fat redistrib · clitoral enlargement (irreversible) · ↑facial/body hair + scalp loss.

5 · MONITORING

Levels + exam at 3 & 6 months, then annually.
