Management of Gout

Acute flare → treat-and-tap · chronic → treat-to-target urate <6. ACR 2020 guideline.

Diagnosis & Mimics

  • Acute flare: abrupt warm, red, exquisitely painful joint ± fever; ~73% at the 1st MTP.
  • Tap before steroids if infection possible — crystals do NOT exclude septic arthritis (they can coexist).

  • Gout = needle, negatively birefringent MSU. Pseudogout = rhomboid, weakly positive CPP (knee >50%).
  • Hemarthrosis = sanguineous fluid.

Modifiable Risk Factors

  • Diet ↑: red meat, shellfish, beer/spirits, fructose. Protective: dairy.
  • Meds ↑: thiazide/loop diuretics, ASA, ACE-I, ARBs (except losartan), ethambutol, PZA, niacin.
  • Prefer losartan + amlodipine for BP (mild uricosuric). Treating HTN/DM/HLD lowers risk.
  • Do NOT stop indicated low-dose aspirin to manage gout (ACR 2020).

Urate-Lowering Therapy (ULT)

Start ULT ifAlso consider after 1 flare if
≥2 flares/yr · tophi · 1 flare + erosionsCKD ≥3 · urate >9 · urolithiasis
  • Allopurinol first-line (all, incl. CKD): start 100 mg/d (≤50 if CKD), titrate q2–4 wk, max ~800.
  • Treat-to-target urate <6 mg/dL (<5 to dissolve tophi).
  • Prophylaxis (low-dose colchicine/NSAID/prednisone) for 3–6 mo until target dose reached.
  • Starting ULT during a flare does NOT worsen it; continue chronic ULT through flares.
  • Febuxostat 2nd-line only — CARES boxed warning (CV/all-cause death).
HLA-B*5801 → allopurinol hypersensitivity (rash, fever, eosinophilia, ↑LFTs, renal failure). Screen Han Chinese / Korean / Thai / African-American ancestry; if positive, do NOT use allopurinol.

Do / AVOID

  • DO arthrocentesis + X-ray for any new hot joint; blood cultures if febrile.
  • DO match the flare drug to comorbidities; pair every ULT start with prophylaxis.
  • AVOID trusting serum urate during a flare — may be falsely low, normal, or high.
  • AVOID steroids during active infection; treat gout after source control.
  • AVOID stopping ULT during a flare.
Pearl: recent skin infection + a hot joint with a high cell count = treat for septic arthritis even when MSU crystals are present.
